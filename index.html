Powerful features

Dexfiat is a community centric financial product, a DeFi gateway developed to ease your financial needs. This allows an easy, reliable, fast and secure access to fiat and cryptocurrencies while enabling p2p fund transfer and also generating revenue to the liquidity providers

Swap & Pool

A swap and liquidity pool that allows users to swap both synthetic fiat to crypto and also crypto to crypto. The pool allow liquidity providers to earn from all the transaction carried out on Dexfiat swap.

Learn more »

Dexfiat Multi-sig wallet

A non-custodial wallet that allows users to securely store their coin and tokens. While enabling instant withdrawal, deposit, 2- factor authentication, wallet backup and other features.

Learn more »

Fiat Dex

A P2P fiat section that enables a decentralized deposit and withdrawal for users.

Learn more »

Governance

We enable a decentralized community easy, reliable, fast and secure access to fiat and cryptocurrencies while generating revenue to the protocol users and enabling borderless fund transfer. We are connecting a para-chain between the fiat and crypto system.

Learn more »

How it Works

Dexfiat will evolve into enabling peer to peer (p2p) funding algorithm, while the Dexfiat smart contract act as an escrow between the buyer and the seller of Dexfiat commodity such as DNGN. It is a completely new mechanism designed for scalability, liquidity, fairness, safety and low volatility. Dexfiat is connecting the fiat and crypto system.
